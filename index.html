About Me

I am a composer with over ten years of experience in writing music. I was born in Cardiff and lived in Wales my whole life. I am have a Bachalor's degree in Music Composition from the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama.

My Work

I have written for a wide range of instrumental groups from solo instruments to full orchestras. I also have experience in writing electronic and ambient music. My main passion is in songwriting where I have developed skills in lyric writing. My work can mostly be found on Youtube and Soundcloud but more platforms will be utilised in the future.
